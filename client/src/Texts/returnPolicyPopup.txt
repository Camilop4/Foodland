
At FOODLAND, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality products and exceptional service. If for any reason you are not satisfied with your purchase, we will be happy to assist you with an exchange or return.

CHANGES

You can exchange any product you have purchased from our store within 30 days of the original purchase date.
Products must be in their original condition, unopened and with all original labels and packaging.
You must present the original purchase receipt or proof of purchase.
Products that do not meet these requirements may not be eligible for an exchange.
RETURNS

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days of the original purchase date.
Products must be in their original condition, unopened and with all original labels and packaging.
You must present the original purchase receipt or proof of purchase.
If the product was purchased with a credit card, the refund will be made to the same credit card used for the purchase. If it was paid in cash, the refund will be made in cash.
Products that do not meet these requirements may not be eligible for a return.
EXCEPTIONS

Perishable items, such as fresh food, are not eligible for an exchange or return.
Products that have been used or damaged after purchase are not eligible for an exchange or return.
Products that were purchased as part of a special or discounted offer may be subject to additional restrictions.
We reserve the right to modify our exchange and return policy at any time without prior notice.
